Tic Tac Toe

Rules:

Players

  • 2 players
  • One is X, the other is O

Turns

  • Players take turns placing their mark in an empty square
  • X usually goes first

Winning Conditions

A player wins if they place three of their marks in:

  • A horizontal row (e.g.,top row: X X X)
  • A vertical column (e.g., middle column: O O O)
  • A diagonal (e.g., top-left to bottom right: X X X)

Draw (Tie)

  • If all 9 squares are filled without a winner, the game is a draw
